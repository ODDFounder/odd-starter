## Boundaries & Limitations

### Scenarios NOT Suitable for ODD

- ❌ Creative exploration (needs flexibility)
- ❌ Pure art projects (subjectivity-driven)
- ❌ Zero-cost prototyping (cost-sensitive)

### Scenarios BEST Suited for ODD

- ✅ Enterprise applications (need auditability)
- ✅ Finance/Healthcare (compliance requirements)
- ✅ Large teams (need standardization)
- ✅ High-risk systems (need responsibility tracing)

> **ODD is NOT designed to maximize innovation speed.**
> **ODD is designed to prevent structural collapse in scaled AI production.**
